Find and Adopt Animals

My website combines two important concerns: finding lost animals and adopting new friends. When you find a lost animal, you can indicate its location and notify other users who will receive real-time updates. Additionally, you can view animals available for adoption, offering the opportunity to provide a new home.

About Me

Hi! I'm the creator of this project, which combines my passion for animals with technology. Through this site, I aim to help reunite lost animals with their owners and provide a platform for adopting animals in need of a home.
